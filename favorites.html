Old Friends

I love these sites and use them frequently. They have served well and are still serving well. Several of them, I have used for many years. Bay 12 Games is home to one of my favorite games of all time and several other great games from the same developers. Crunchyroll is one of the largest collections of legal, English-subtitled anime. Google holds all the random information on the interwebs, and Reddit has some pretty hilarious subs.

Reliable Newcomers

There are a few of these sites, however, that only recently appeared more often on my list. Stack Overflow, for example, is a great source of programming help. itch.io is wonderful for indie developers and gamers alike. GitHub is a repository cloud service for programs and libraries. LinkedIn is a more formal social platform; it is used to follow professionals, track resumes, and get jobs.
